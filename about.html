About Us

The Smart Recovery: Lost & Found Tracking System is a student-friendly platform created to help you report lost belongings, post found items, and reunite them with their rightful owners.

Whether it's keys, ID cards, accessories, books, or gadgets — this portal acts as a central hub for your campus community to stay connected and help each other.

Our mission is simple: To make returning lost items effortless, trustworthy, and fast for everyone.
